OCR Project

This project focuses on Optical Character Recognition (OCR), combining image processing techniques and artificial intelligence implemented in C. The goal was to develop a system capable of analyzing images and extracting text with high accuracy. We implemented algorithms to preprocess images (such as noise reduction, binarization, and segmentation) and trained a simple neural network to recognize characters. The project was a great introduction to low-level AI programming and gave us valuable insights into how OCR systems work.
